Fall 2018:

Through the Sports Analytics Group at Berkeley (SAGB), I worked on a consulting team for Cal Football. We created an improved Fourth-Down decision making model for the coaches to reference in-game. Some of my responsibilities included writing an algorithm to identify the n most similar offenses/defenses over the past k years given a certain opponent, using this filtered data to adjust our model for any given opponent, building web scrapers to collect data, and creating heatmaps of historical trends in Fourth-Down decision making and success rates.
